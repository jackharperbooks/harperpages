Welcome, Welcome!

Step into my library. Every book here is a piece of my journey, a pound of my flesh—sealed with literary flourish. And now, shared freely, because tariffs suck, Pumpkin Spice Palpatine can take a seat, and that Hillbilly Elegy nitwit isn’t worth the ink. Writing, for me, has always been therapy—a way to wrestle with my own mental health struggles. And let’s be real: The Catty Bitches Club of Facebook’s MM romance scene is more interested in draining wallets than telling stories. Here at HarperPages, my mission is different: to give you my backlist titles FREE OF CHARGE, along with new worlds I’ve yet to unearth. Consider it my token of gratitude for readers like you.
